Our Engineering Capabilities

1. Professional 3D Scanning

Don't spend hours measuring with calipers. We use advanced scanning to capture the "Point Cloud" of any physical object, creating a 0.03mm accurate digital twin.

2. Reverse Engineering

Have a broken automotive clip or a discontinued part? We scan the remains and reconstruct a "clean" CAD model that is often stronger than the original.

3. Print-Ready CAD Optimization

A beautiful model doesn't always print well. We design specifically for Additive Manufacturing, ensuring watertight meshes and optimal layer strength.

Professional File Formats
