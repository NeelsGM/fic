THE FUTURE IS LIGHTWEIGHT

At Forged in Carbon, we don't just manufacture parts; we engineer performance. Based in Durban, we specialize in the convergence of additive manufacturing and composite materials.

Whether you need a custom intake manifold for a track car, a complex architectural model, or a high-strength carbon fiber component using our advanced 3D printed mold technology, we bring the digital world into physical reality.

  • Automotive Grade Precision
  • Rapid Prototyping & Iteration
  • Bespoke One-off Components

OUR CAPABILITIES

Bridging the gap between concept and carbon.

3D Design & Modeling

From napkin sketches to CAD perfection. We design complex organic shapes and mechanical assemblies optimized for manufacturing.

Precision 3D Printing

Utilizing industrial-grade filaments to create robust functional parts, jigs, and fixtures. High resolution, dimensional accuracy.
