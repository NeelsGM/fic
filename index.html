Why Forged Carbon?

Traditional "Woven" carbon is limited by the fabric's direction. We forge complex shapes that were previously impossible.

Isotropic Strength in all directions
Extreme Internal Complexity
Unique Stealth-Marbled Finish
By combining 3D Printed sacrificial tooling with compression molding, we produce aerospace-grade components with rapid-iteration speed.

This is the new standard for Durban's performance automotive and industrial sectors.
