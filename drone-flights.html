The Evolution: Gaussian Splatting

Gaussian Splatting is a paradigm shift in 3D capture. Unlike traditional photogrammetry, which builds rigid surface meshes, this process renders scenes using millions of tiny 3D points, resulting in near-photorealistic fidelity.
